Mission

To advocate for digital security and literacy for Small Businesses across Nairobi — ensuring they can leverage technology to protect themselves from cyber vulnerability.

"Empowering Nairobi's SMEs Through ShieldOS"

Problem

Nairobi's SMEs face a rising tide of cyberattacks with zero budget, no IT staff, and no awareness to defend themselves.

Field Insight "We can't afford IT guys. We just hope we don't get hacked."
— Retail Owner, Nairobi Market
  • 60%+ of SMEs reported a breach in the last 3 years
  • 43% of all cyberattacks target small businesses
  • Kenya lost KSh 29.9B to cybercrime in 2025
  • 60% of breached SMEs close within 6 months
  • 0% of SMEs we interviewed had a security budget

ShieldOS

A hardened Linux operating system that installs on any refurbished PC via a USB drive. No IT engineer needed. No subscription. No internet required to run.

Designed from the ground up for Nairobi's small business owners — people with no technical background who just need their business to stay safe.

🛡️
Phishing Blocker
Blocks known phishing domains at the OS level — before the browser even loads.
🔐
Full Disk Encryption
Business data encrypted by default. If the PC is stolen, the data is safe.
📋
App Whitelisting
Only approved applications run. Ransomware and malware cannot execute.
🔄
Auto Security Updates
Security patches applied automatically when internet is available.
📖
Built-in SME Guide
Plain-language security manual pre-installed — no internet needed to read it.
